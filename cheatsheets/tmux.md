## panes
### split

```%``` split vertically

```"``` split horizontally

```x``` close current

### switch

```→ ← ↑ ↓``` switch pane

```;``` last active pane

```q + num``` switch

```q``` show numbers


### move

```{``` move current left

```}``` move current right

### resize

```hold b + → ← ↑ ↓``` or ```ctrl + → ← ↑ ↓```

### misc

```!``` convert to window


## sessions

```s``` show all

```$``` rename

```d``` detach

```tmux kill-session -a``` kill all but current
